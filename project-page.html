Overview

Medical reports are often written for clinicians rather than everyday users. This project focuses on simplifying dense medical language into understandable summaries while preserving important context. Instead of aggressive compression, the system prioritizes clarity and interpretability — ensuring summaries feel informative rather than oversimplified.

Problem

Patients frequently struggle to interpret medical terminology, which can lead to confusion or anxiety. Reports are information-rich but not user-friendly. Key challenges addressed: • Technical medical jargon • Information overload • Low readability for non-specialists This creates a gap between available information and practical understanding.

  • Technical Terminology
  • Information Overload
  • Poor Readability
  • User-friendly UI

Impact

The system revealed meaningful listening patterns that help understand user engagement and preference shifts. These insights can guide recommendation improvements and strategic decision-making.
